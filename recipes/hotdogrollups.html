Hotdog Roll-Ups Recipe

Description

Hotdog roll-ups are a quick and easy snack or meal option that combines the classic flavors of hotdogs with a crispy, cheesy exterior.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of white bread
  • 2 tbsps of yellow mustard
  • 4 thin slices of sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 8 beef hot dogs
  • 2 tbsps + 2tsps of sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce
  • 1/4 cup of mayo
  • 1/2 tspns of seasoned salt
